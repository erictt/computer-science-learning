  • Let A∈Rm×k and B∈Rk×n.(AB)T=BTAT.A \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times k} \text{ and }B \in \mathbb{R}^{k \times n}. (AB)^T = B^T A^T.A∈Rm×k and B∈Rk×n.(AB)T=BTAT.
  • Let A,B and CA,B \text{ and } CA,B and C be conformal matrices so that ABCABCABC is well-defined. Then (ABC)T=CTBTAT(ABC)^T = C^T B^T A^T(ABC)T=CTBTAT.
